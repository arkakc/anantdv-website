About Anantdv

Human intelligence, engineered into business systems.

Anantdv is a technology solutions partner focused on ERPNext, Frappe customization, AI automation, business applications, websites, cloud infrastructure and strategic consulting. We build practical digital systems that help teams work with more control, visibility and speed.

7+Years of delivery experience across ERP, software and digital transformation.
30+Specialists working across ERPNext, development, infrastructure and support.
APACExperience across India and Asia-Pacific business environments.
ERPCore focus on customized ERPNext solutions, not generic software installation.

Our origin is simple: technology should empower people.

We do not treat software as a replacement for people. Our approach is built around Human Intelligence: understanding how teams actually work, then using ERP, automation and cloud systems to remove repetitive work, reduce mistakes and improve decision-making.

We build systems around real business workflows.

Every business has its own approvals, reports, documents, teams, locations and operating habits. That is why we focus on process mapping, configuration, customization and adoption instead of forcing a rigid software template on the client.

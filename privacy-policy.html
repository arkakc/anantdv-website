Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us.

This Privacy Policy explains how Anantdv collects, uses, stores and protects information when you visit our website, contact us, or communicate with us about ERPNext, automation, web, cloud, infrastructure or consulting services.

Last updated: 22 June 2026

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide when you contact us, submit an enquiry, send an email, call us, or communicate through social media.

  • Name, company name and job role
  • Email address and phone number
  • Project requirements, service interests and message details
  • Any files, notes or business information you choose to share with us

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to respond to enquiries, understand your requirements, prepare proposals, provide support, improve our website and communicate about relevant services.

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Cookies, Logs & Analytics

Our website may use basic browser features, hosting logs, analytics tools or third-party services to understand website performance, traffic and user experience. These tools may collect technical information such as browser type, device type, pages visited, referring website and approximate location.

If analytics or marketing tools are added in the future, they should be reviewed and configured responsibly.

4. Information Sharing

We may share information only when necessary to deliver services, respond to your request, comply with legal obligations, protect our rights, or work with trusted service providers such as hosting, email, form processing, analytics or cloud service providers.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration or loss. However, no internet-based system is completely risk-free.

Please do not send passwords, secret keys, payment credentials or highly sensitive information through a public website contact form.

6. Data Retention

We retain enquiry and communication information only as long as reasonably needed for business, support, project, legal or administrative purposes. When information is no longer required, we may delete or anonymize it.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites such as LinkedIn, Facebook, hosting providers or external tools. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites.

8. Your Choices

You may contact us to request access, correction or deletion of information you have shared with us, subject to business and legal requirements. You may also choose not to provide optional information.

9. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised update date.
